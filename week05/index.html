'Nerd,''Nonsmoker,''Wrongdoer': How Might A.I. Label You?

Honestly, I think I got the message about AI and face recognition being biased. Like who the heck gets to decide what these programs identify people as. What right do they have? I don't think a lot of people know about this kind of bias though. I think I want to inform more people about this, especially since my friend did not know about the Imagenet website. AI face recognition is used in a lot places and potentially I think it can be harmful or can be used to mistreat certain groups of people.
